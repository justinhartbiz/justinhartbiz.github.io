Boomers/Day Turning 65

The Voice of the Silver Tsunami

Every day, 10,000 Baby Boomers turn 65. By 2030, one in five Americans will be retirement age. This isn't a statistic—it's a seismic shift that will reshape families, communities, and the real estate market for decades.

Paul Williamson has spent over a decade on the front lines of this transition. As CEO and Founder of True Legacy Homes, he's helped more than 2,000 California families navigate the emotional and logistical challenges of senior transitions, inherited properties, and estate sales.

Now, through his podcast The Legacy Playbook and his speaking engagements, Paul shares the hard-won lessons from thousands of family conversations—offering clarity for adult children, practical guidance for professionals, and a compassionate framework for an industry that too often treats these moments as mere transactions.
