üìã Actionable Recommendations (Evidence-Backed)

1. Use [firstname] tokens. Only universal positive predictor. Small effect but free to implement. r=+0.041 overall, positive in all 3 clients.
2. Keep openers 50-150 characters. Under 30 chars underperforms (z=-0.054). Over 150 chars crashes (z=-0.178). Sweet spot: 51-70 chars (z=+0.065).
3. Don't over-punctuate. Fewer exclamation marks correlate with better performance across all clients. "BREAKING!" is a loser pattern.
4. Skip questions in openers. Negative correlation in all 3 clients. Statements outperform questions.
5. Focus on body copy, not opener tricks. Opener features explain <1% of performance variance. The remaining 99%+ is body copy, targeting, timing, and landing page.
