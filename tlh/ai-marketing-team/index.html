How Agents Work

1
Trigger

Schedule, event, or human request kicks off the agent

2
Execute

Agent gathers data, creates content, or takes action

3
Review

Draft goes to staging for human approval (when needed)

4
Publish

Approved work goes live automatically

Example: Blog Post Workflow

Monday 8 AM ‚Üí Orchestrator dispatches "Write blog on probate timeline"
Monday 8:05 AM ‚Üí Content Agent researches topic, writes draft with SEO
Monday 8:10 AM ‚Üí Draft posted to WordPress as "Pending Review"
Monday 8:11 AM ‚Üí Justin gets notification with preview link
Monday 10:30 AM ‚Üí Justin replies "approved" or "change the intro"
Monday 10:31 AM ‚Üí Agent publishes (or revises and resubmits)

Key Principles

  • Human-in-the-loop: Sensitive actions require approval before going live
  • Transparent: Every action is logged; you can see what agents did and why
  • Interruptible: Agents can be paused or overridden at any time
  • Auditable: Full history of all decisions and outputs
