Mission & The Economic Truth

True Legacy Homes helps families navigate life's hardest transitions ‚Äî the death of a loved one, a parent's move to care, the letting go of a family home ‚Äî with dignity, speed, and fairness.

TLH is a home acquisition company disguised as an estate sale company.

  • Externally: TLH is the one trusted partner for estate transitions
  • Internally: Estate sales are the visible entry product. The profit engine is home purchase (buy ‚Üí renovate ‚Üí flip)
  • Estate sale: ~$10K (7% of LTV) ‚Ä¢ Home purchase: ~$140K+ (93% of LTV)
