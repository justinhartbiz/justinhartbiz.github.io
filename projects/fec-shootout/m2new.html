üí° Actionable Takeaways for Fundraising Strategists

1. Time Your Appeals to Electoral News Cycles

Data shows small-dollar giving spikes 125% within days of electoral victories. Launch "victory" fundraising appeals immediately after positive election news‚Äînot days later.

2. Democrats' Small-Dollar Base is More "Event-Driven"

The DNC's unitemized giving fluctuated 2.5x more dramatically than RNC's. If you're advising Democrats, prepare rapid-response fundraising infrastructure. If advising Republicans, focus on consistent monthly giving programs.

3. Post-Election Period is Critical

November 2025 generated more than double the monthly average for DNC. The "election high" donor psychology creates a window of opportunity‚Äîcapitalize with strong ask.

4. RNC Has More Stable Small-Dollar Engine

While DNC had bigger spikes, RNC's small-dollar giving was steadier month-to-month (CV: 10% vs 26%). RNC may benefit from drip campaigns; DNC from burst campaigns.

5. Year-End is Dual-Purpose

December saw DNC unitemized drop 46% but total receipts stay strong‚Äîindicating donors shifted to tax-advantaged year-end max gifts. Have both small and large-dollar asks ready.
