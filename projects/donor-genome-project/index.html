The Universal Arc

Our analysis reveals a consistent 5-part structure across high-performing creatives regardless of client, cause, or channel.

1

Hook (0-30 chars)

Personalization + emotional trigger or curiosity gap

2

Story (30-150 chars)

1-2 sentences of specific detail with named person, specific date, or concrete image

3

Bridge (emotional pivot)

Connect story to donor's ability to act

4

Ask (single amount)

$25 for nonprofit, verification/action for political

5

CTA (with arrows)

">> [link]" or "[action] >> [link]" (one link only)
