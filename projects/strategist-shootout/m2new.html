Strategic Recommendations

🛑
STOP: "Thank You in Advance"
This phrase triggers psychological reactance and actively suppresses donations. Audit all existing templates immediately. This is costing you 15-20% of potential conversions.
🚀
START: Named Matching Donors
The single highest-ROI change: replace "your gift will be doubled" with "Sarah M. will match your gift." This alone could generate +39% more donations with zero additional spend.
💡
MYTH: Identity-Specific Framing
Conventional wisdom says "make it personal." Research shows specific identity framing (e.g., "help a Roma child") can HALVE donations vs generic appeals. Broad, universal narratives work best in SMS.
