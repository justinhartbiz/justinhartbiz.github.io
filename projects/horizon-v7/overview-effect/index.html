Overview Effect

The documented cognitive shift astronauts experience seeing Earth from space: borders dissolve, fragility sharpens, and identity expands from nation to planet.

Why it matters

This is not a metaphor. Astronauts repeatedly report a measurable perspective collapse of tribal concerns when Earth is seen as a single shared biosphere suspended in darkness.

Question: If this shift can be induced without orbit, what does that imply for education, policy, and conflict resolution?
