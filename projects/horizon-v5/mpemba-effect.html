Proposed Mechanisms

Evaporation reduces hot water mass. Convection currents form differently. Dissolved gases escape. Supercooling is easier. None is universally accepted.

Why It's Disputed

The effect is highly sensitive to container shape, water purity, freezer air flow, and initial conditions. Many labs reproduce it; many can't. Replication is the whole problem.

2016 Study (Burridge)

A Cambridge study found no robust evidence for the Mpemba effect under controlled conditions ‚Äî reigniting debate. Other teams still claim to observe it consistently.

The Reframe

A schoolboy's question in 1969 still doesn't have a settled answer in 2024. Science doesn't always resolve cleanly ‚Äî sometimes the most reproducible result is the disagreement itself.
