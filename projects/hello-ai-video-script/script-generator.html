AI Explainer Format

1. Attention-Grabbing Intro (15-20 sec) - Provocative question or surprising fact - Clear problem statement 2. Value Proposition (20-30 sec) - What viewers will learn - Why it matters to them 3. Concept Explanation (60-90 sec) - Clear, jargon-free explanation - Real-world analogies - Visual metaphors 4. Practical Applications (60-90 sec) - 3-5 concrete use cases - Examples relevant to target audience - Before/after scenarios 5. Limitations & Considerations (30-45 sec) - Honest assessment of constraints - Common pitfalls to avoid - Ethical considerations when relevant 6. Actionable Takeaway (20-30 sec) - Specific next step viewers can take - Resource recommendations - Call to action Total: 3-5 minutes (500-800 words)
