# Week in Review Dashboard

A comprehensive weekly snapshot of key metrics, activities, and insights across all domains (DonorBureau, TLH, Rational Ground, AI Operations). This dashboard identifies what's working, what needs attention, and where the highest leverage opportunities exist for the upcoming week.

## Key Features

### 1. Cross-Domain Intelligence
Brings together metrics from all areas of operation to provide a unified view of performance and opportunities. Breaks down silos between different business units.

### 2. Priority Alert System
Highlights the most critical issues requiring immediate attention, from revenue crashes to content gaps, using a color-coded severity system.

### 3. Performance by Domain
Visualizes performance across all domains with week-over-week comparisons, making it easy to spot trends and changes.

### 4. Autonomous Build Tracker
Shows all recent autonomous builds with descriptions and value assessments, providing accountability for AI initiatives.

### 5. Opportunity Prioritization
Ranks opportunities by potential impact and implementation effort, helping focus on the highest-leverage actions first.

### 6. Content Gap Analysis
Identifies scheduling gaps and content imbalances across platforms to prevent missed publication windows.

### 7. Quick Wins List
Provides a prioritized list of high-impact, low-effort actions that can be implemented immediately.

### 8. Next Week Planning
Structured view of the upcoming week with prioritized actions, monitoring requirements, and expected outcomes.
